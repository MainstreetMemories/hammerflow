## Getting Started

### Logging In
Simply open the app in your browser. Your data saves automatically to your browser.

### Navigation
Use the sidebar on the left to switch between:
- **Dashboard** - Overview of your business
- **Leads** - Potential customers
- **Customers** - Confirmed customers
- **Jobs** - Active roofing jobs
- **Estimates** - Quotes you've sent
- **Invoices** - Payments owed
- **Inventory** - Materials tracking
- **Reports** - Financial overview
- **Admin** - Vendors, sales team, expenses

## Lead Management

### Adding a New Lead
1. Click **+ Add Lead** or use the quick action button
2. Fill in the customer's information:
   - **Name** - Customer's name
   - **Phone** - Contact number
   - **Email** - Email address
   - **Property Address** - The property for the job
   - **Lead Source** - How they found you (Google, Referral, etc.)
   - **Follow Up** - When to contact them
   - **Notes** - Any important information
3. Click **Save Lead**

### Converting a Lead
Leads can be converted in two ways:
- **→ Customer** - Just converts them to a customer record
- **→ Job** - Creates both a customer AND a job in one step

Click the appropriate button next to the lead to convert.

### Editing a Lead
Click **Edit** next to any lead to modify its information.

## Customer Management

### Adding a Customer
1. Go to the **Customers** page
2. Click **+ Add Customer**
3. Fill in name, phone, email, and address
4. Click **Save Customer**

### Viewing Customer History
Each customer shows:
- Number of jobs completed
- Total amount spent
- Contact information

## Job Tracking

### Creating a Job
1. Click **+ Add Job** on the Jobs page
2. Fill in:
   - **Customer** - Select or type customer name
   - **Address** - Job location
   - **Type** - Repair, Replacement, etc.
   - **Status** - New, Scheduled, In Progress, Completed
   - **Start Date** - When work begins
   - **Value** - Job cost/value
   - **Crew** - Who's working on it
   - **Notes** - Any special instructions
3. Click **Save Job**

### Job Statuses
- **New** - Just created
- **Scheduled** - Date set to start
- **In Progress** - Work underway
- **Completed** - Job done
- **On Hold** - Waiting

### Editing Jobs
Click **Edit** next to any job to update its status, crew, or details.

## Contracts & Scanning

### Scanning a Contract
The **📄 Scan Contract** button (glowing yellow) lets you upload a photo or PDF of a signed contract:

1. Click the Scan Contract button
2. Choose a file (image or PDF)
3. The AI will extract:
   - Customer name
   - Phone number
   - Address
   - Contract price
   - Shingle type
   - And more!
4. Review the extracted data
5. Click OK to save to your jobs

### How It Works
The app uses AI to read the contract and automatically fills in the job details - no manual entry needed!

## Weather Forecasting

### Checking Weather
The dashboard shows a 7-day weather forecast to help you plan jobs:

- **Green days** - Good for roofing
- **Yellow/orange** - Caution needed
- **Red** - Rain - consider rescheduling

The app automatically detects your location, or you can click **Refresh** to update.

## Tips & Tricks

### Data Saves Automatically
Everything saves to your browser automatically. No need to manually save!

### Pipeline Workflow
The natural flow is:
1. **Lead comes in** → Add as Lead
2. **They agree** → Convert to Customer (or straight to Job)
3. **Work begins** → Job created with all their info
4. **Job done** → Invoice created, payment tracked

### Weather Check Before Scheduling
Always check the 7-day forecast before scheduling jobs. Rain days are highlighted in orange/red.

### Use the Search
Every page has a search bar - quickly find any lead, customer, or job.

### Quick Actions
The buttons at the top of the dashboard let you quickly add:
- New Lead
- New Customer  
- New Job
- New Invoice
- Scan Contract
